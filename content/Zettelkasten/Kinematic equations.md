# Kinematic equations
Assuming constant acceleration (const a)
1. $v = v_0 + a \cdot t$
2. $\Delta x = v_0 \cdot t + \frac{1}{2} \cdot a \cdot t^2$
3. $v^2 = v_0^2 + 2 a \Delta x$
4. $\bar{v}t = \Delta x$
	- $\Delta x = \frac{v_0 + v}{2} \cdot t$
